## Concept

A structured AI companion with:
- Daily check-in prompts ("How was your day?")
- Mood tracking with streak history
- Journaling with AI reflections
- Weekly summaries generated by AI
- Optional: planning/advice mode

**Not** an open-ended chatbot (competing with Character.AI/Replika is dumb). This is a structured journaling wrapper that *uses* AI.
