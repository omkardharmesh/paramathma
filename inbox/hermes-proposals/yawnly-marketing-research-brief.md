### 6. Realistic outcome at ₹10,000

**The math to verify:**
- If CPI = ₹15-30 (parenting category estimate)
- ₹10,000 / ₹25 = ~400 installs maximum from pure ads
- Subtract 30-40% for ad platform learning/inefficiency = ~250-350 installs
- Need 2-4% to subscribe (₹99/mo) to break even in 3-4 months
- **Verify this CPI estimate against actual dev reports**
